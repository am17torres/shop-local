Local should be easier than ordering online — not harder

Today, buying local means calling around, checking five different sites, or just giving up and ordering national. We fix that with one storefront for the whole town.

🔎

One search for everything

Search "8-inch terracotta planter" or "local honey" and see results from every shop in town at once — not twelve tabs, not a single phone call.

🛒

One cart, every shop

Planter from the garden shop, jam from the grocer, a hammer from the hardware store — all in a single cart and one checkout.

⚡

Same-day, not someday

A neighbor drives a batched afternoon loop and delivers the same day. It's already in your town — why wait two days for a truck from out of state?

When you buy here, the town keeps the money

Every order supports a family-owned shop and a local driver — not a warehouse three states away. Same convenience as the big sites, but the dollars stay on Main Street.

🏪

Real shops, real people

You're buying from the same shops you'd walk into — now open online, with the inventory you'd actually find on their shelves.

🚐

A neighbor delivers it

Deliveries run on one batched local loop driven by someone from town. Faster turnaround, lower footprint, money that stays here.

🔒

Never charged for what's not there

Your card is only authorized at checkout. Nothing is charged until the shop confirms the item is on the shelf — so you never pay for an out-of-stock guess.
