You shouldn't need a tech team to sell online

Big platforms ask you to re-key your whole inventory, learn new software, and ship boxes nationwide. That's not Burnt Hills. Here's the deal instead.

🗂️

We build your catalog

Send us a list, a spreadsheet, or just let us photograph the shelf. We set up and maintain your listings — not you.

📲

You just confirm orders

An order pings your phone. You tap Confirm if it's in stock, or Reject if it's not. That's the whole job.

🚐

We handle delivery

A local driver picks up on a batched afternoon loop and delivers same-day. No shipping, no packing peanuts.

The part shop owners actually worry about

"What if I sell something I don't actually have?" It can't happen here. The customer's card is only authorized at checkout — a temporary hold. We don't charge it until you confirm the item is on the shelf.

1

3

4
