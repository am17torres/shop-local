Frequently asked questions

The stuff shop owners usually ask before signing on.

What if a customer cancels or I reject an order?

You pay nothing. The platform fee only applies to orders that clear your confirmation step and are fulfilled. If you reject, the customer's hold is released and nobody is charged.

When do I get paid?

Stripe Connect pays out to your bank account on a rolling basis — typically 2 business days after a confirmed order. You'll get a Stripe dashboard to track every payout.

Are there any setup or monthly fees?

None. We cover the cost of building your catalog, setting up your listing, and keeping things current. The only thing we take is the per-order percentage on sales that ship.

How is my tier decided?

By how much revenue you've earned through the platform so far this calendar year — not your shop's overall sales. You start every year in Tier 1 at 1%, and only move up a tier once your cumulative sales through us cross each threshold. The tier resets every January 1st.

Is there a ceiling on what I'll pay?

Yes. Each tier has a hard annual cap — $100 at Tier 1, $2,000 at Tier 2, $30,000 at Tier 3, $400,000 at Tier 4. Once you hit the cap for your tier, you pay no more platform fees that year until you cross into the next tier.

Does the delivery fee affect what I earn?

No. Delivery is billed to the customer at $1 per mile from your shop to their address, on top of the order subtotal, and goes directly to the driver. Your payout is calculated only on the subtotal — and customers who pick up in store pay no delivery fee at all.
